Top 3 Soccer Players

Here are our opinions on the Top 3 Soccer Players based on stats and accomplishments.

Lionel Messi

Alongside Cristiano Ronaldo, Lionel Messi has written history in the football books for years to come. His technique, dribbling, pace, and vision have chracterized him as one of the best playmakers ever. He makes every pass, look, and touch, seem so easy at such a professional level. Since almost 15 years people have been comparing him to the next player in this list, even though they have very different gamestyles.

Cristiano Ronaldo

The other best player in football history that, as well as Messi, has broken so many records in the last decade. Him and Messi have been the best players in the world for more than 13 years already, which is something that had never been done before in the history of this sport. Something that characterizes Cristiano as a world-class player is his power, physical, shooting, and determination when it comes to important games or moments.

Neymar Junior

The one who was once called the "Joia", which means "gem" in portuguese. People called him like that at the time in Brazil referring to his bright future, since he was so young yet so good at the sport. Neymar has some of the best skills and playmaking ability ever seen. Although he has not always been this type of player, he has matured enough to turn into one of the best playmakers there is nowadays. His flair, skills, vision, and reaction speed are his main strong attributes as a player.
